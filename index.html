    About this prototype. Built to test the design of the lab dashboard. All data shown is illustrative, calibrated against current market conditions (June 2026) but not drawn from live feeds. In production, signals would be pulled from named indices: ICE Brent, ICE TTF, S&P Platts JKM, WTO Strait of Hormuz Trade Tracker, Caldara-Iacoviello Geopolitical Risk Index, Drewry World Container Index, ASX 200 VIX, NAB Monthly Business Survey, AEMO Quarterly Energy Dynamics, ABS Capex Intentions Survey, RBA. A consumer-goods tile is deliberately not included; no clean single-index signal exists for this sector and the team should decide whether to add a proxy or leave the gap honest.
